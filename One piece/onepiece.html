About One Piece

One Piece is a Japanese manga series written and illustrated by Eiichiro Oda. It has been serialized in Shueisha's Weekly Shōnen Jump magazine since July 1997, with its individual chapters compiled into 98 tankōbon volumes as of February 2021.

Akainu (real name: Sakazuki) is one of the main antagonists of the anime/manga, One Piece. He was one the 3 Marine Admirals and one of the most powerful fighters the Marines have to offer. He is currently the Fleet Admiral as Sengoku's successor.

The Protagonist

Monkey D. Luffy is the series' main protagonist, a young pirate who wishes to succeed Gold Roger, the deceased King of the Pirates, by finding his treasure, the "One Piece".
