Jujutsu Kaisen

About Jujutsu Kaisen

Jujutsu Kaisen is a Japanese manga series written and illustrated by Gege Akutami, serialized in Shueisha's Weekly Shōnen Jump since March 2018. The individual chapters of Jujutsu Kaisen are collected and published by Shueisha, with fifteen tankōbon volumes released as of March 2021.

Plot

The Villains

Suguro Geto is one of the few Jutsu Sorcerers that have earned the respect of Satoru Gojo. His cursed technique allows him to control cursed spirits, but the extent of his power has yet to be demonstrated on screen. It could very well be the case that Suguro is the man behind each of the unregistered special grade cursed spirit's actions. The fact that this sorcerer is powerful enough to barter with these special-grade curses is impressive. If it is revealed that he has been controlling them, Suguro may be on an entirely different level than viewers first assumed.

The antagonist

Sukuna. Sukuna is one of the main antagonists of the manga series Jujutsu Kaisen. He was a powerful Jujutsu sorcerer who was known as the King of Curses. Upon his death his power and consciousness was sealed into his 20 fingers.
