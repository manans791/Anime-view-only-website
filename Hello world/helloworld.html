About Hellow World

Hello World is a 2019 Japanese animated sci-fi romantic drama film directed by Tomohiko Itō and produced by Graphinica. It was released in Japan on September 20, 2019. Its cast include Takumi Kitamura, Tōri Matsuzaka, and Minami Hamabe.

The Villains

The antagonist is nothing but a AI machine created by humans to store the recorded data as memories in its never ending/infinite Storage.
